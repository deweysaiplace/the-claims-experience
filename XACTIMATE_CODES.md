## Common O&P Rules (Overhead & Profit)

- **Applicable**: Jobs requiring a GC to coordinate 3+ trades
- **Standard rate**: 10% overhead + 10% profit (20% combined)
- **State Farm position**: Generally applies on large-loss multi-trade claims
- **Contractor disputes**: Always document why O&P applies with trade count

---

## ACV vs RCV Quick Reference

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `RCV` | Replacement Cost Value — full replacement at today's cost |
| `ACV` | Actual Cash Value — RCV minus depreciation |
| `Recoverable Depreciation` | Difference between ACV and RCV (released on completion) |
| `Non-recoverable Depreciation` | Applied to items past useful life or per policy |
